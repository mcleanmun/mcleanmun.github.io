Heran Essayas is a senior at McLean High School who will be your co-chair for the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) committee. She’s been participating in MUN since her freshman year, so she’s glad to be involved in McMUNC VI. As someone who frequently volunteers for a poverty-centered non-profit, Heran is excited to see some of the solutions that delegates bring to the conference! Outside of MUN, you can find Heran playing on the McLean field hockey team, spending countless hours working on The Highlander (McLean’s student-run newsmagazine) or trying to beat her time record in sudoku. She hopes that this committee will be a fruitful and memorable experience for everyone! Please feel free to reach out to her with any questions at heranessayas@gmail.com.
