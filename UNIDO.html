Vice-Chair

Aleena Gul

Aleena Gul is currently a junior at McLean High School and is thrilled to be your vice-chair for UNIDO. She began MUN in sixth grade, captivated by the interesting debates and unique lessons MUN offers. She has a keen interest in global affairs and wants to fully participate in MUN activities in the future as well. Aleena hopes that the conference provides a welcoming environment for delegates to bring unique ideas to the table and participate in interesting debates. In addition to MUN, Aleena is the News editor for McLean’s student publication, The Highlander, and is an active member of the Muslim Student Association, McLean Debate, and Science National Honors Society. In her free time, she enjoys painting, spending time with family, and hanging out with friends. Aleena is looking forward to an exciting committee and hopes that it opens doors for unique ideas, diplomacy, and cooperation among delegates while tackling these crucial issues around the globe. Should you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact Aleena Gul at aleena9933@gmail.com.
