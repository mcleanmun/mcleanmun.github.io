Vice-Chair

Eve Ginsberg

Eve Ginsberg is a junior at McLean High School and is excited to be vice-chairing for the Federal Reserve (2008) committee. Having been a member since seventh grade, Model UN has been Eve’s favorite club and she has served as a crisis staffer and vice-chair for McMUNC in previous years. She also participates in Youth Advisory Council, Girls Leadership Committee, Girls Who Code, and several other extra curriculars. Eve loves to bake, travel (she has now been to ten countries), read, and watch Netflix. Eve is hoping to pursue a career in finance or computer science. Eve is proud to say that she has read all there is to know about the 2008 housing bubble, watched the Big Short (which she highly recommends), and can’t wait to be your vice-chair. If you need, you can contact her at egginsberg10@gmail.com.
