Co-Chair

Brittany “Mimi” Peng

Brittany "Mimi" Peng is a senior at McLean and currently serves as McLean MUN's Director of Logistics. She has been doing MUN since her freshman year where she developed the quintessential skills of being a leader and agent for change. Hoping to pursue a future in politics or law, one of Brittany's biggest dreams is to become a Congresswoman to push agendas that will fulfill the needs of the people she is serving and reinvigorate the democratic principles that make up the fabric of the United States. Something that Brittany would like to touch upon is the particular drive for awards in Model UN. While it may seem that awards are a key part of competing in Model UN, Brittany would like you all to take away the tokens of experience you gain as a Model UN delegate at McMUNC VI and all your future conferences. The lessons you learn and the skills you cultivate in being a Model UN delegate are far more valuable than the awards you may receive: they are transforming and empowering. If you have any questions, please reach Brittany at brittanyjp021@gmail.com.
